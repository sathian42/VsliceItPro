About V-Slice Pro 4U

No Uploading: Since the tool works directly in your browser, you don't waste data or time uploading 2GB+ files to a server.

100% Private: Your video never leaves your computer. Your files are processed locally, ensuring no one else can see your content.

Language Support: The system is specially designed to render South Indian languages like Tamil, Telugu, Malayalam, and Kannada perfectly without any character errors.

Safe & Fast: Enjoy a clean environment for creators with no viruses, no hidden tracking, and no annoying pop-up ads.

How to Use

1. Enter Details: Type your Movie Title and choose a Prefix (like "Part" or "Episode") to organize your clips.

2. Customize Text: Use the checkboxes to decide if the Movie Title or Part Number should be visible on the video frames.

3. Pick Your Style: Select a Font from the dropdown that supports your language (Tamil, Telugu, English, etc.) to avoid broken characters.

4. Set Time & Ratio: Choose the length of each segment (e.g., 60 seconds) and select the Aspect Ratio—Vertical (9:16) for Reels/TikTok or Wide (16:9).

5. Skip Unwanted Parts: To remove ads or intros, enter the time ranges in the Omit box (e.g., 00:00:00 - 00:00:20).

6. Select Video: Click the "Choose Video File" button to load a movie from your local storage. It stays 100% private.

7. Start: Press "START PROCESSING." The tool will slice and automatically download each part to your computer.

Fix Problems

1. Page Crashing or Freezing: Processing large videos requires a lot of RAM. Close other tabs and background apps, then refresh.

2. "SharedArrayBuffer" Error: Ensure you are using Chrome/Edge and have uploaded the coi-serviceworker.js file.

3. Missing Downloads: Check the address bar for a "Pop-up blocked" icon and select "Always allow."

4. Text Showing as Boxes: Select MasterFonts or a specific language font from the dropdown.

5. Slow Processing: Video encoding depends on your CPU. Avoid heavy software while V-Slice is running.

6. Mobile Device Issues: Best used on Desktop Chrome. iPhones may block background processing.

7. Final Reset: Clear browser cache or use an Incognito Window.
